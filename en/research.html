About

Malong Research is a group of world-class research scientists and engineers working to advance state-of-the-art computer vision research and deploy new technologies at scale. The group contributes to the international scientific community by publishing papers, releasing datasets/challenges, giving academic talks, and collaborating with global universities and other corporate research groups, including Google Research and Microsoft Research Asia.

Dr. Weilin Huang


The group is led by Chief Scientist Dr. Weilin Huang. Prior to Malong, he was a post-doctoral scholar and researcher at VGG of the University of Oxford. Dr. Huang published the world’s top performance on numerous vision benchmarks including MIT Place205, MIT Indoor67, and ICDAR13-15.

Dr. Hsiao-Wuen Hon


Malong is proud to have computer scientist and AI luminary Dr. Hsiao-Wuen Hon as a member of its Technical Advisory Board. Dr. Hsiao-Wuen Hon is corporate vice president of Microsoft, chairman of Microsoft’s Asia-Pacific R&D Group, and managing director of Microsoft Research Asia. He drives Microsoft’s strategy for research and development activities in the Asia-Pacific region, as well as collaborations with academia.

Dr. Yi Ma


Malong is also proud to have computer vision luminary Professor Yi Ma as a member of its Technical Advisory Board. Professor Yi Ma, of University of California, Berkeley, is an ACM Fellow, IEEE Fellow and one of the "Top 50 of the World’s Most Influential Authors in Computer Science" ranked by Semantic Scholar and reported by Science Magazine.
